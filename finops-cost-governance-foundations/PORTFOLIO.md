## What this portfolio proves
- Cloud cost can be governed as a **financial control domain**
- Variance triggers drive **action**, not dashboards
- Decisions are **documented, justified, and traceable**
- Evidence is retained in a way suitable for leadership, finance, and audit review

---

## What this portfolio intentionally does NOT claim
- No production tenant ownership
- No IAM or platform admin authority
- No on-call or operational responsibility
- No proprietary or employer billing data
