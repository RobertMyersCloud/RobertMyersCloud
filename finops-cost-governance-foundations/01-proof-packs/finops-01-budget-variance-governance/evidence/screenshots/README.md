## Purpose
These screenshots are retained as **supporting evidence artifacts** for **Proof Pack 01 — Budget vs Actuals & Variance Governance**.

They demonstrate:
- baseline budget context
- actual spend accumulation
- service-level driver analysis
- forecasted month-end exposure

---

## Evidence standards
All screenshots are:
- captured from **Azure Cost Management**
- time-bound to the stated billing period
- referenced by **Evidence ID (EV-YYYY-MM-XXX)**
- linked from the **Evidence Index**

No screenshots imply:
- production authority
- engineering control
- ownership of an employer tenant
