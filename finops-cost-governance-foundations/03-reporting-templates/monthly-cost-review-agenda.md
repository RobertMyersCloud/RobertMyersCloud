## Purpose
Standardize the monthly cloud cost review meeting so outcomes are consistent:
- variances are explained
- decisions are captured
- owners and due dates are assigned
- evidence references are recorded

---

## Meeting details
- **Billing period:** YYYY-MM
- **Date/time:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Facilitator:** (FinOps / IT Finance)
- **Attendees:** (Finance, Cost Owners, Governance)

---

## Pre-read (required)
- Variance table (latest)
- Evidence index (updated)
- Executive brief (one-pager), if used

---

## Agenda (30–45 minutes)

### 1) Open (2–3 minutes)
- Confirm period, scope, and decision thresholds

### 2) Spend summary vs budget (5 minutes)
- Budget vs actual headline
- Trend vs prior month (directional)

### 3) Investigate items (10–15 minutes)
For each Investigate item:
- driver + impact horizon
- recommended action
- confirm evidence references

### 4) Escalate items (10–15 minutes)
For each Escalate item:
- confirm driver and materiality
- capture decision (approve / re-allocate / reduce / defer)
- assign owner and due date
- confirm evidence references

### 5) Allocation exceptions (5 minutes)
- Unallocated spend amount and trend
- resolution plan and owner

### 6) Forecast note (3–5 minutes)
- forecast changes and rationale
- any known scope changes

### 7) Close-out (2 minutes)
- recap decisions, owners, due dates
- confirm evidence index updated

---

## Outputs (what gets saved)
- Updated variance table + commentary references
- Decisions captured (owner + due date)
- Evidence index updated (EV / VC IDs)
- Executive brief (optional)
